AgentGuard Website Audit

A simple tool that combines classic website safety checks with AI-agent exposure checks. It helps website owners understand headers, cookies, forms, scripts, chatbots, hidden prompt-like text, and AI policy files such as llms.txt.

Run website audit

Classic security

Checks HTTPS, security headers, cookies, forms, mixed content, and exposed technology.

AI exposure

Looks for chatbot scripts, third-party scripts, hidden prompt-like text, llms.txt, and robots.txt.

Plain-English report

Explains what was found, why it matters, and what should be fixed first.
